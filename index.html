The Project

mWork enables “clients” to create micro-tasks that require human intelligence and receive the results of those tasks by paying a small fee.
On the other end, anyone with a smartphone, especially those in the developing world, can complete those tasks in return for small payment.
Our team has undergone the design process to construct an interface that completes both sides of the equation.
All steps of the design process, including reports, presentations, and prototypes, may be found below.
